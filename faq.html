FAQs


Q: How do I create an account?

A: Click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the homepage. Fill in the required details and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email to activate your account.


Q: How do I change my account information?

A: Log in to your account and go to the "My Account" section. Here you can update your personal details, address, and password.


Q: What should I do if I forget my password?

A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a link to reset your password.

Orders

Q: How do I place an order?

A: Browse through our products, select the items you wish to purchase, and add them to your cart. Once you are ready, click on the cart icon and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to complete your purchase.


Q: Can I modify or cancel my order?

A: You can modify or cancel your order before it is shipped. Go to the "My Orders" section in your account, select the order you want to modify or cancel, and follow the instructions.


Q: How can I track my order?

A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the carrier's website.

Shipping


Q: What are the shipping charges?

A: Shipping charges vary based on the delivery location and the weight of the package. You can view the shipping charges during the checkout process before completing your order.


Q: How long will it take to receive my order?

A: Delivery times depend on your location and the shipping method chosen. Standard delivery usually takes 3-7 business days, while expedited delivery options are available at an additional cost.


Q: Do you offer international shipping?

A: Yes, we offer international shipping to selected countries. Please check the shipping options available during checkout.


Returns and Refunds


Q: What is your return policy?

A: We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Visit the "Returns" section on our website for more details on how to initiate a return


Q: How do I return an item?

A: Log in to your account and go to the "My Orders" section. Select the order containing the item you want to return, and follow the instructions to initiate a return. We will provide a return shipping label for your convenience.


Q: When will I receive my refund?

A: Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item. The refund will be credited to your original method of payment.

Payment


Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and net banking. You can select your preferred payment method during the checkout process.


Q: Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

A: Yes, we use secure encryption technology to protect your personal and payment information. Your data is safe with us.


Q: Can I use multiple payment methods for a single order?

A: Currently, we do not support multiple payment methods for a single order. You will need to choose one payment method during checkout.
